# Pēpeha

```
Ko Mana tōku maunga
Ko Aroha te moana
Ko Whānau tōku waka
Ko au e tu atu nei

Mana is my mountain
Aroha is my sea
Whānau is my waka
And all of that is me

Ahakoa pāmamao
Kei kōnei koe

Though you are far away
I hold you near

Keep the home fires burning
So you see clear
Kia maumahara mai rā
No kōnei koe

Ko Mana tōku maunga
Ko Aroha te moana
Ko Whānau tōku waka
Ko au e tu atu nei
```
